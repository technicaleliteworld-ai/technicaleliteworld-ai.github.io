Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application StoryRead (referred to as "the App"), created by our development team. The App is designed as an interactive reading application for novels and stories.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better reading experience, analyze app performance, and support the App via advertising and purchases.

Personal Information

When you log in using third-party authentication services (such as Google Sign-In), we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture URL

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App, including:

  • Device Information (such as device model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • IP address and network status
  • App usage statistics (pages read, reading duration, app opens)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To personalize your reading experience and synchronize your reading progress across devices.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions (such as Ad-Free plans).
  • To monitor app performance, fix crashes, and improve functionality.
  • To display relevant ads (such as AdMob banner, interstitial, and open ads).
  • To send you transactional notifications (such as subscription confirmations) or push notifications about new stories.

3. Third-Party Services and Analytics

The App integrates with third-party service providers that collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. These include:

  • Google Play Services: Used to run the application environment.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging): Used to monitor app usage, track crash logs, and deliver push notifications. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Facebook SDK & App Events: Used to track app installs and optimize advertising campaigns. Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Used for secure user authentication.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your local app preferences, bookmarks, and reading history are stored securely on your device using local storage databases (such as Hive and Shared Preferences). Account data is synced securely with our backend servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, but please be aware that no security system is completely impenetrable.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Data Deletion Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data. To request data deletion, you can contact us at our support email listed below. We will process your request within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
